Issue: National Finance Remodeling（國家財政重塑）

Chinese Summary（中文摘要）

AI 與機器人取代大量勞動後，國家財政將從「勞動稅制」轉向「機器人稅 × 數據稅 × 自動化效率稅」。人口減少將降低部分社福壓力，但高齡化會推升醫療支出。未來財政的核心將是：如何重新分配 AI 所創造的超額產能，並投入在全民再教育與高科技醫療。

English Summary（英文摘要）

As AI and robots replace labor, national finance will shift from a labor-based tax structure to robot taxes, data taxes, and automation-efficiency taxation. Population decline reduces welfare burdens, but aging increases healthcare costs. The central fiscal task becomes reallocating the surplus productivity created by AI toward universal re-education and advanced healthcare systems.
