Issue: Educational System Restructuring（教育體系重組）

Chinese Summary（中文摘要）

教育需要全面重塑，從「知識記憶型」轉向「高階認知 × 創造力 × AI 協作能力」。未來人才需要能跨領域思考，理解 AI 的限制與倫理，並能與機器協作。基礎數據素養應全面普及，而頂端教育必須強化 AI 理論、哲學、系統設計等深層能力，以形成文明競爭優勢。

English Summary（英文摘要）

Education must be restructured from a “knowledge-memorization model” to a system prioritizing higher-order cognition, creativity, and AI collaboration skills. Future talent must think across domains, understand AI limitations and ethics, and work effectively with automated systems. Data literacy should be universal, while elite education must strengthen AI theory, philosophy, and systems design to build a civilizational advantage.
