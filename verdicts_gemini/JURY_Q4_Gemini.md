Issue: China’s AI Development Path（中國的 AI 發展路徑）

Chinese Summary（中文摘要）

在少子化、外來移民不足、社會階層固定的情況下，中國更可能走向「以政府為主導的集中式發展模式」。集中治理能快速整合算力、資料與標準，提升系統效率。然而，缺乏多元人才與自由移動，可能造成 AI 系統創新能力不足，形成速度快但創造性有限的結構。

English Summary（英文摘要）

With declining birth rates, limited immigration, and rigid social stratification, China is likely to pursue a government-led centralized AI development model. Centralization enables rapid allocation of compute, datasets, and standards, increasing system efficiency. However, limited diversity and restricted mobility may hinder breakthrough innovation, creating a model that is fast but less creative.
