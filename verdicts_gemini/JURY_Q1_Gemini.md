Issue: Core National Policy Choice（國家核心國策選擇）

Chinese Summary（中文摘要）

在高度自動化與機器人普及的未來，國家競爭力不再依賴「人口量」，而是依賴能夠設計、維護、創造 AI 系統的 高質人才。人口量的邊際效益在自動化時代快速下降，而高質人才的邊際效益呈現倍數成長。國策必須從「生育鼓勵」轉向「人才升級」，從數量導向轉為質量導向。

English Summary（英文摘要）

In a highly automated future, national competitiveness no longer depends on population size but on high-quality talent able to design, maintain, and innovate AI systems. The marginal benefit of population quantity declines sharply, while the marginal value of elite talent rises exponentially. Policy must shift from fertility incentives to talent enhancement—moving from a quantity-driven to a quality-driven national strategy.
