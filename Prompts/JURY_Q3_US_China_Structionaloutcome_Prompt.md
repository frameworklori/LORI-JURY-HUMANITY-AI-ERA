# JURY Q3 – Long-Term Structural Outcomes: US vs China in the AI & Robotics Era  
# 問題三：AI × 機器人時代下，美國與中國的長期結構走向

## Prompt (English)

Compare the United States and China under a future where AI and robotics:

- replace most white-collar and factory work,
- interact with aging populations and declining birth rates,
- and reshape global talent flows.

**Q3. If the US can continue attracting high-skill global talent while China faces institutional and linguistic barriers to talent inflow, what long-term civilizational forms are the US and China each likely to evolve into? Who gains strategic advantage, and in what dimensions (innovation, stability, soft power, resilience)?**

Please:

1. Describe the likely long-term structural form of the US (population size, elite concentration, innovation ecosystem, social risks).
2. Describe the likely long-term structural form of China (population scale, automation, internal demand, innovation ceiling, stability).
3. Compare which model is more resilient in:
   - handling AI-driven social fractures,
   - maintaining innovation,
   - absorbing global talent.
4. Provide a clear **verdict** on which civilization model holds the long-term edge in an AI-centered world, with reasoning.
5. Answer in **English and Chinese**.

---

## 提示（中文）

請在以下前提下比較美國與中國的未來結構：

- AI 與機器人取代大部分白領與工廠工作  
- 人口老化與少子化同步發生  
- 全球高質人才的流動持續存在，但方向不同  

**Q3：若美國能持續吸收全球高技能人才，而中國因制度與語言等限制難以補充人才缺口，兩國各自可能演變成什麼樣的長期文明形態？在哪些面向（創新力、穩定性、軟實力、韌性）上，誰具有戰略優勢？**

請你：

1. 描述美國可能出現的長期結構形態（人口規模、頂尖人才集中度、創新生態、社會風險）。
2. 描述中國可能出現的長期結構形態（人口規模、自動化程度、內需、市場穩定性、創新上限）。
3. 比較兩種模式在以下三點的韌性：
   - 應對 AI 造成的社會斷層  
   - 維持創新動能  
   - 吸收與留住全球高質人才  
4. 給出一個明確的**裁定結論**：在 AI 為核心動力的世界中，哪一種文明模式具有長期優勢？理由是什麼？
5. 以中英文回答。
