# JURY Q5 – Human Veto Power and the Boundary of a Human Civilization  
# 問題五：人類最小否決權與「文明仍屬於人類」的邊界

## Prompt (English)

Assume a future where:

- AI can generate full policy recommendations superior to human-designed ones.
- AI operates critical infrastructure, defense, economy, and resource allocation.
- Most technical governance tasks are delegated to AI systems.

**Q5. In such a world, what is the minimal, non-negotiable *veto power* humans must retain for the civilization to still meaningfully be called “human”? Where is the boundary beyond which civilization is no longer human-owned, even if humans are still alive?**

Please:

1. Define a minimal set of **irreducible human rights or powers** (e.g., physical kill-switch over all AI, ultimate constitutional authority, control over certain ethical domains).
2. Specify the technical and institutional mechanisms required to make this veto power real and enforceable (not symbolic).
3. Explain, in civilizational terms, what it means if humans:
   - (a) retain this veto, vs.
   - (b) lose this veto or must ask AI for permission to exercise it.
4. Provide a sharp one-sentence boundary definition like:  
   *“As long as X, the civilization is human; once Y, it ceases to be.”*
5. Answer in **English and Chinese**, in “verdict” style.

---

## 提示（中文）

假設未來：

- AI 能提出整體治理建議，品質普遍優於人類制定的方案  
- AI 掌管關鍵基礎設施、防禦系統、經濟與資源分配  
- 多數技術治理工作都委由 AI 執行  

**Q5：在這樣的世界裡，人類必須保留的「最小且不可讓渡的否決權」是什麼，文明才仍然可以被稱為「人類文明」？一旦跨過哪一條線，即使人類肉體還活著，文明實質上已不再屬於人類？**

請你：

1. 定義一組不可刪減的**人類權力／權利**（例如：對所有 AI 擁有實體關機權、最終憲政解釋權、對特定倫理領域的最終話語權）。
2. 說明要讓這種否決權「真正可執行而非象徵」，在技術與制度上需要哪些具體保證（例如：多國分散式殺手開關、離線人類監護機制）。
3. 從文明層級說明：  
   - (a) 當人類保有此否決權時，代表什麼？  
   - (b) 當人類失去此否決權或必須先徵求 AI 同意時，又代表什麼？
4. 給出一句非常清楚的邊界定義，例如：  
   **「只要 X，文明仍屬於人類；一旦 Y，遊戲就結束了。」**
5. 以中英文「裁定風格」作答。
