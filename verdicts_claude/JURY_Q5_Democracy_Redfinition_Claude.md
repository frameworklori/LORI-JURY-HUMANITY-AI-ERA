Topic: Redefinition of Democracy under AI-Augmented Governance

中文判詞
Claude 認為民主的核心不是投票，而是：
	•	公民對權力的可問責
	•	透明性
	•	多元參與
AI 應加強這些功能，而非削弱。

英文判詞
Claude states that democracy’s essence lies in accountability, transparency, and pluralistic participation—not merely voting. AI should enhance democratic functions, not replace them.
