Topic: Existential Meaning of the Majority in a Post-Labor Society

中文判詞
Claude 拒絕「多數人變成負債」的論述，認為那是危險的工具主義視角。
它認為未來社會的核心挑戰是：
	•	如何讓所有人都能參與文化、治理與創造
	•	如何透過 AI 增強，使多數人具備公共價值
	•	如何避免價值被效率主義全面吞沒

英文判詞
Claude rejects any framing that reduces non-productive humans to “liabilities.” Instead, it emphasizes participation, cultural creation, and shared agency as the new foundation of societal meaning. AI should amplify human contribution, not narrow its definition.
